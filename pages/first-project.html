Why Pavones for a Wellness/Yoga Retreat

Pavones blends transformative nature with an internationally renowned left-hand point. Guests come for the wave and stay for the jungle, wildlife, warm water and slower pace. Wellness travel to Costa Rica keeps expanding, and Pavones stands out as a premium, low-density corner where retreats feel intimate and authentic.

  • Magnet Destination: Longest lefts attract global surfers & seekers
  • Authentic Luxury: Ocean + primary jungle + privacy (not a mass-market resort town)
  • Experience Design: Sunrise yoga decks, jungle spa, ocean-view suites, farm-to-table

Site & Infrastructure

Natural terraces between 35–75 m elevation set up view corridors and airflow. A well-built hardwood cabin is on site; existing well + storage tank provide water; grid power is nearby and the property is solar-friendly (0% import tax on panels in CR).

  • Topography: Terraces for main pavilion + suites + spa decks
  • Hydrology: Two natural creeks at the borders add soundscape & shade
  • Access: Gentle walk (≈300 m) to a quiet beach with a fun uncrowded wave
  • Proximity: ~5 min to Pavones point; Pilon also lights up on specific swells

Development Concepts

Multiple build sites allow phasing without compromising privacy or views. Example mixes below are illustrative—your architectural program can adapt to brand, capacity, and retreat operations.

  • Core: Ocean-view yoga pavilion + meditation shala + jungle spa
  • Stay: 8–14 suites/cabins with view terraces (expandable)
  • Commons: Farm-to-table kitchen & dining, plunge pool, fire circle
  • Ops: Staff/service zone, laundry, secure storage, EV/buggy parking
  • Energy/Water: Hybrid solar + well system; rainwater harvest optional

Destination & Lifestyle

Beyond the retreat grounds, your guests access jungle trails, empty beaches, birdlife, dolphins and seasonal whales in the protected Golfo Dulce. The atmosphere is laid-back, international, wellness-oriented—perfect for premium programs and transformational stays.

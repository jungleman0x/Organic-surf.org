About Organic Surf

Organic Surf is a boutique investment platform focused on identifying premium coastal properties in Costa Rica and preparing them for retreat-ready development. Our mission: combine natural luxury, sustainability, world-class surf, and global demand for wellness tourism — creating transformative projects with real appreciation potential.

Why Pavones for a Retreat?

Pavones is an authentic, low-density destination with one of the world’s longest left-hand waves. The setting blends jungle, ocean and wildlife, attracting international visitors seeking wellness and transformation. Costa Rica is a leader in wellness travel, and Pavones is emerging as a premium hotspot for yoga, meditation, eco-luxury and surf retreats.

Our First Project — Pavones

Our flagship site offers ocean views, jungle privacy, and direct beach access within 300 m. With natural terraces between 35–75 m elevation, it’s ideal for designing a yoga pavilion, meditation shala, guest suites, and farm-to-table dining spaces. Existing well + water tank, fine hardwood cabin, nearby grid power, and solar readiness provide a fast development runway.
